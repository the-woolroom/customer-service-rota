Shift patterns

Define each desk shift (office, hours, lunch) and how many people you need on it, per day of the week. Go to Weekly Rota and click Auto-assign week to fill the rota from these patterns — it rotates who gets each shift fairly and skips anyone already marked Holiday/Off. Row order is priority — if there aren't enough people on a day, patterns higher up get filled first. Use ↑/↓ to reorder.
